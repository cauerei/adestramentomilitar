# **App Name**: Military Dog Training

## Core Features:

- Video Course Access: Provides access to a series of video lessons teaching the military method of dog training.
- Digital Book: Offers a comprehensive PDF ebook containing detailed information and supplementary materials to support the video course.
- Training Checklists: Provides structured checklists to guide users through the training process, ensuring consistent and effective implementation of techniques.
- Bonus Content Delivery: Distributes recipes for natural dog food and a guide for managing canine anxiety as bonus resources to enhance the overall training experience.
- Community Access: Allows access to a private group for course participants, offering a platform for discussions, support, and shared experiences.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Military Green (#556B2F) to convey authority and a connection to the military theme.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), creating a neutral, authoritative, yet soft canvas.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF8C00) used for calls to action, providing high visibility and encouraging user engagement.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, computerized, and techy feel, fitting for headlines and short text amounts.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a grotesque-style with a neutral and modern look for body text.
- Use of strong, vector-based icons in shades of orange and green to represent different modules and features of the training program.
- The layout should be structured with clear sections, ample spacing, and prominent CTAs to guide the user through the sales page effectively.
